about
Hi, my name is Danielle and I like to draw stuff.

I am a student based in Wellington, New Zealand studying a Bachelor of Creative Media Production at Massey. A majority of my work consists of traditional inked drawings, but I love experimenting with art digitally. I am currently working to gain more skills in animation, VFX and 3d modelling.

Now, I will blabber on about something to fill the space. Long ago, the four nations lived together in harmony. But everything changed when the fire nation attacked. Only the Avatar, master of all four elements could stop them.
